Encoding
========

`UTF-8 <https://www.unicode.org/faq/utf_bom.html#UTF8>`__ validation and
codepoint counting facilities.

.. admonition:: Info

   To use declarations from this page, include the ``<essence/encoding.hpp>`` header.

.. cpp:namespace-push:: essence

.. cpp:function:: usize encoding::count(std::string_view s)

   Count the UTF-8 codepoints in a string. May also be called
   with a ``const char*`` or ``const std::string&``

   .. rubric:: Example

   .. code-block:: cpp

      std::string micro { "µ" };

      assert(micro.length() == 2);
      assert(encoding::count(micro) == 1);

.. cpp:function:: bool encoding::validate(std::string_view s)

   Determine UTF-8 validity of a string. May also be called
   with a ``const char*`` or ``const std::string&``
